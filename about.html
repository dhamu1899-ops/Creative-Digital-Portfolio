Stackly started in 2015 as a two-person design practice in Salem. Ten years later we're still small on purpose — a stacked team of designers, engineers and strategists who work directly with the founders and product leads who hire us.

Our story

Why we work
in layers

Most agencies hand your project between departments — strategy, design, build, growth — losing context at every handoff. Stackly stacks those disciplines into one team, so the person who wrote your brand strategy is in the room when the interface gets built.
