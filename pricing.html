How long does a typical engagement run?

Starter engagements run four to six weeks. Studio engagements are ongoing monthly retainers, typically three to six months for a full product build. Partner engagements are open-ended.

Can we switch plans later?

Yes — most clients start on Starter to validate direction, then move to Studio once the roadmap is clear. We'll help you figure out the right time to scale up.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

About a third of our client base is pre-seed to Series A. We offer flexible scheduling and equity-inclusive arrangements for the right early-stage teams.

What's not included in these prices?

Third-party costs like hosting, paid media spend, stock licensing and app store fees are billed separately at cost, with no markup.
